About Me

I'm a Process Engineer and Analyst, with over 5 years of experience in Process analysis, Energy, Retail, Tech and Research industries. I posses extensive experience in analysing processes, detecting bottlenecks and area of redundancy. I am proficiency in BizAgi, BPMN 2.0, Python, Tableau, Power BI, Excel, and SQL.

I obtained my first degree in Mechanical Engineering followed by a Masters in the same Field. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD at the University of Oldenburg, Germany.

I love working behind the scenes of both big and small business decisions knowing that I'm a huge influence in decision making process which is not based on opinions but entirely on metrics and insights.

Outside of work, I love to visit the beach, travel, seeing movies and at times playing football with my friends.
