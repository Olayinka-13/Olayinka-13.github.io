Summer Booking Dashboard

In this project, I developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze the summer booking data for a hotel. The dashboard focuses on key metrics such as percentage of cancellations, average daily rate and total revenue lost on a monthly basis. Using an intuitive calendar design, the dashboard provides a visual representation of the booking trends and enables stakeholders to easily identify patterns and insights.

USA Favourite travel destination by numbers of passengers

This report showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides insights into the most popular international travel destinations for US travellers. The dashboard features live data refresh and presents key metrics, including the top 10 most visited destinations, yearly top destinations, and passenger counts on a weekly, monthly, and quarterly basis. Utilizing the capabilities of Power BI, the report incorporates DAX calculations to analyze and visualize the data effectively.

HR Employees Insight

The dashboard created serves as a powerful tool for HR analytics, enabling me to efficiently analyze and manage employee data. Through this interactive dashboard, I can easily determine the number of inactive employees, active employees, and distinguish between voluntary and involuntary attritions. By applying various filters and drill-down options, I can identify patterns and trends related to employee turnover, helping me make informed decisions and devise strategies to improve employee retention.

Onyx Data Challenge Dashboard (2023)

This comprehensive dashboard provides valuable insights into various aspects of the Onyx data challenge. It offers an in-depth analysis of hotel revenue and cancellation losses, comparing monthly cancellation percentages to average daily rates (ADR) to gauge their impact and many more. Users can also filter data across hotels (city and resort) to explore specific metrics for each property, enhancing their understanding of revenue patterns and cancellation trends in different hotel types. The dashboard also displays the refresh rate, which shows the precise date and time of the last data update. This can be used to refresh the dashboard as well as update the data. With interactive features and valuable data trends, the dashboard empowers users to optimize resources, improve customer experience, and make data-driven decisions tailored to each property's unique characteristics.
